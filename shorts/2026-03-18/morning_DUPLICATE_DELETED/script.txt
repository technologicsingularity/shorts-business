That click when you take a photo? It's completely fake. Real digital cameras haven't needed mechanical shutters for years. The sound is just a tiny speaker playing a WAV file. Why? Because we expect it. Early phones added the noise so people knew a picture was taken. Some countries legally require it to prevent secret photography. Your iPhone lets you turn it off in silent mode. But that satisfying mechanical sound? Pure theater. Photography has been silent for decades. The click is just nostalgia, packaged and played through a speaker.
